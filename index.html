Our Mission & Vision

Creating a Healing and Empowering Environment

WellCity is a first-of-its-kind, holistic, Christ-centered wellness event designed to serve young adults ages 18–33 by equipping them in the five core areas of human flourishing: Spiritual, Mental, Physical, Social, and Financial.

We believe young adults today are facing unprecedented challenges: post-pandemic isolation, anxiety, cultural confusion, financial uncertainty, and a longing for authentic connection and purpose.

Through WellCity, we're building a community that will help young adults restore the biblical model of shalom by bringing Christ at the center in every chore of our EKLESIA Healing model.
